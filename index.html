How to Use InfoScope

  1. Enter your search query in the input box. You can use phrases (enclosed in quotes) and logical operators (AND, OR).
  2. Select your preferred ranking model: TF-IDF or BM25-lite.
  3. Toggle field boosts to prioritize certain fields like title or tags in the ranking.
  4. Click the "Search" button to retrieve relevant articles from the dataset.
  5. Review the search results displayed, which include article titles, dates, sources, abstracts, and tags.
  6. Click on any article title to view its detailed page.
  7. Use the "Reset" button to clear your search and start over.
